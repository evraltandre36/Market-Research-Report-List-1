<p><p>The medical grade seal market comprises various types, including O-rings, gaskets, lip seals, and others. O-rings are circular seals that provide a tight seal between two surfaces, preventing fluid leakage. Gaskets are flat seals used between two surfaces to ensure airtight or watertight connections. Lip seals feature a flexible lip that maintains contact with a shaft to block contaminants. Other seals may include custom designs or advanced materials, catering to diverse medical applications that require high reliability and sterility.</p></p>
