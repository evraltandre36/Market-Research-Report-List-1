<p><p>The Industrial Nitrogen Gas market primarily comprises two types: Compressed Gas and Liquid Nitrogen. Compressed Nitrogen Gas is stored in high-pressure cylinders and is widely used in applications like welding, food packaging, and chemical manufacturing, offering ease of transport and use. Liquid Nitrogen, on the other hand, is stored at extremely low temperatures and is utilized in cryogenic freezing, pharmaceuticals, and industrial processes requiring low temperatures. Both forms cater to diverse industrial needs and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
