<p><p>Crossflow filtration is a membrane filtration process where the feed liquid flows parallel to the membrane surface. This technique minimizes fouling and enhances the efficiency of separation, making it particularly valuable in various applications such as wastewater treatment, food and beverage processing, pharmaceuticals, and biotechnology. Unlike traditional filtration methods, crossflow filtration allows for continuous processing, enabling improved productivity and reduced operational costs.</p><p>The Crossflow Filtrations Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.8% during the forecast period.
