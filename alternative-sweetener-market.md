<p><strong>The Alternative Sweetener Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-intensity Sweetener (LIS)</li><li>High-intensity Sweetener (HIS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The alternative sweetener market comprises two main types: low-intensity sweeteners (LIS) and high-intensity sweeteners (HIS). Low-intensity sweeteners provide a milder sweetness and are often derived from natural sources like sugar, offering fewer calories, making them popular in beverages and baked goods. In contrast, high-intensity sweeteners deliver a much stronger sweetness with minimal or no calories, commonly used in diet products and sugar-free options. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers seeking to reduce sugar intake.</p></p>
