<p><p>The Terbium Sputtering Target market can be categorized into two main types: Rotary Type and Non-Rotatable Type. Rotary Type targets are designed to enhance uniformity and efficiency in material deposition by allowing continuous rotation during the sputtering process, which increases target lifespan and optimizes performance. In contrast, Non-Rotatable Type targets remain stationary, often providing simpler setups and lower costs but may experience more uneven wear. Both types serve critical roles in various applications, including electronics and optical coatings.</p></p>
