<p><p>The refrigerator temperature logger market is segmented into two main types: wireless temperature loggers and wired temperature loggers. Wireless temperature loggers utilize Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, or cellular networks to transmit data remotely, enabling real-time monitoring and alerts without physical connections. In contrast, wired temperature loggers rely on direct connections, typically using cables to transfer data, which may limit mobility but can offer more stable connections in certain environments. Both types are essential for ensuring proper refrigeration and compliance in various industries.</p></p>
