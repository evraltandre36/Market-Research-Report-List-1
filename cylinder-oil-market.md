<p><strong>The Cylinder Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-BN</li><li>Mid-BN</li><li>Low-BN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cylinder Oil market is categorized into three main types based on the base number (BN) of the oil, which indicates its alkalinity. High-BN oils, typically above 70 BN, are designed for engines operating on high-sulfur fuels, providing better protection against acid formation. Mid-BN oils, ranging from 50 to 70 BN, offer a balance suitable for various operating conditions. Low-BN oils, below 50 BN, are intended for use in low-sulfur fuel environments, where less alkalinity is required for effective engine protection.</p></p>
