<p><p>The fiber-based packaging market is primarily categorized into virgin fiber and recycled fiber. Virgin fiber packaging utilizes newly sourced, high-quality natural fibers, offering superior strength and freshness, making it ideal for premium products. In contrast, recycled fiber packaging is derived from post-consumer materials, promoting sustainability and reducing waste. While recycled fiber is generally more economical and environmentally friendly, it may have varying strength and quality. Both types are increasingly adopted in various industries to meet consumer demand for eco-conscious packaging solutions.</p></p>
