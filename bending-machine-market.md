<p><p>The bending machine market comprises four main types: hydraulic, mechanical, electric, and pneumatic. Hydraulic bending machines use fluid power for precise control and high force, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Mechanical machines utilize gears and levers for bending, often offering high speed but lower precision. Electric bending machines leverage electric motors for energy efficiency and accuracy, ideal for smaller tasks. Pneumatic machines use compressed air, providing quick operation and versatility, typically used in lighter applications. Each type caters to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
