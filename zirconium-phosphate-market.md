<p><p>The Zirconium Phosphate (ZrP) market encompasses three main types: Normal ZrP, Nano ZrP, and Medical ZrP. Normal ZrP is used primarily in ceramics and catalysts due to its stability and ion-exchange properties. Nano ZrP offers unique properties at the nanoscale, enhancing its utility in advanced materials and coatings. Medical ZrP is specifically tailored for biomedical applications, such as drug delivery and bioactive implants, benefiting from its biocompatibility and ability to facilitate targeted therapies.</p></p>
