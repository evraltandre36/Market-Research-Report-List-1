<p><p>The petcoke market consists of various types, primarily including sponge coke, needle coke, and others. Sponge coke is porous and primarily used in aluminum and steel production due to its low sulfur content. Needle coke, characterized by its high carbon content and electrical conductivity, is essential for making electrodes in electric arc furnaces. Other types include shot coke, which is used in the cement industry, and calcined coke, utilized for anodes in aluminum manufacturing. Each type serves distinct industrial applications based on its unique properties.</p></p>
