<p><p>The stroke management market encompasses various diagnostic imaging and monitoring technologies. Computed Tomography (CT) scans provide rapid brain imaging to assess stroke types and damage. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) offers detailed images of brain tissue. Carotid ultrasounds evaluate blood flow and blockage in carotid arteries. Cerebral angiography visualizes blood vessels in the brain. Electrocardiography (ECG) monitors heart activity for arrhythmias linked to stroke risk. Echocardiography examines heart structures and function, while other modalities may include emerging technologies and innovative diagnostic tools.</p></p>
