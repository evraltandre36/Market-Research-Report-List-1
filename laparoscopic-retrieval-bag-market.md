<p><p>The laparoscopic retrieval bag market is categorized into two main types: manual opening and automatic opening bags. Manual opening bags require the surgeon to physically open the bag to collect tissue specimens, offering precision and control during procedures. In contrast, automatic opening bags utilize a mechanism that opens upon deployment, enhancing efficiency by allowing for quicker specimen retrieval. Both types cater to varying surgeon preferences and procedural needs, ultimately improving surgical outcomes in minimally invasive surgeries.</p></p>
