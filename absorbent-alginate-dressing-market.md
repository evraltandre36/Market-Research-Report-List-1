<p><p>The absorbent alginate dressing market is categorized into two main types: adhesive and non-adhesive alginate dressings. Adhesive alginate dressings are designed with a self-adhesive layer that ensures secure application to the wound, providing enhanced stability and ease of use. In contrast, non-adhesive alginate dressings are typically used with a secondary dressing to hold them in place, offering flexibility for managing various wound types without causing trauma upon removal. Both types are widely utilized for their high absorbency and promotion of a moist wound healing environment.</p></p>
