<p><ul><li>Dehydrated Garlic Flakes</li><li>Dried Garlic Granules</li><li>Dried Garlic Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dehydrated garlic market comprises three main types: dehydrated garlic flakes, dried garlic granules, and dried garlic powder. Dehydrated garlic flakes are sliced pieces of garlic that retain a mild flavor, suitable for seasoning and garnishing. Dried garlic granules are coarser than flakes and provide a stronger taste, ideal for seasoning blends and marinades. Dried garlic powder is a fine, concentrated form that offers intense flavor, commonly used in sauces, soups, and various culinary applications. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and usages.</p></p>
