<p><p>The power tool battery market comprises several types, each with distinct characteristics. Lithium-ion batteries are lightweight, have a high energy density, and offer longer runtimes, making them popular in modern tools. Ni-Cad batteries, known for their durability and ability to perform at low temperatures, are often found in older models, though they have largely fallen out of favor due to environmental concerns. NiMH batteries provide a balance between performance and environmental impact. Other batteries may include lead-acid or specialized chemistries for niche applications.</p></p>
