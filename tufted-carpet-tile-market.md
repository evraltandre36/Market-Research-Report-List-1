<p><p>The tufted carpet tile market comprises three main types: cut pile, loop pile, and cut and loop pile. Cut pile features upright fibers that create a soft, plush surface, ideal for comfort. Loop pile consists of loops of yarn, offering durability and a more textured appearance, making it suitable for high-traffic areas. Cut and loop pile combines both techniques, providing a unique aesthetic with varying textures, enhancing both style and functionality. Each type caters to different preferences and usage scenarios.</p></p>
