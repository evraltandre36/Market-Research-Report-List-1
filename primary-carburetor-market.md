<p><strong>The Primary Carburetor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Float-Feed Carburetor</li><li>Diaphragm Carburetor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The primary carburetor market is mainly categorized into two types: float-feed carburetors and diaphragm carburetors. Float-feed carburetors utilize a float mechanism to regulate fuel flow, ensuring a stable mixture for internal combustion engines. They are commonly found in older vehicles and small engines. Diaphragm carburetors, on the other hand, employ a flexible diaphragm to control fuel delivery, making them ideal for applications like lawn equipment and two-stroke engines, where precise fuel metering is essential for performance.</p></p>
