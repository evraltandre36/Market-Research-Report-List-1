<p><strong>The Air Bearing Cylinder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Action</li><li>Double Action</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air bearing cylinder market is primarily segmented into single action and double action types. Single action air bearing cylinders utilize air pressure to produce motion in one direction, relying on a spring or gravity for return. In contrast, double action air bearing cylinders generate motion in both directions using air pressure, allowing for more versatile applications. This differentiation affects their usage in various industries, with single action cylinders often preferred for simpler tasks and double action types for applications requiring bidirectional movement.</p></p>
