Medium phenyl silicone oils strike a balance between performance and cost, providing enhanced thermal and oxidative stability. High phenyl silicone oils, with a greater phenyl content, deliver superior heat resistance, electrical insulation, and viscosity properties, making them ideal for specialized applications in various industries.</p></p>
