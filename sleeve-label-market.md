<p><p>Sleeve labels encompass various types used for packaging. Heat shrink labels contract when heated, conforming tightly around products for a snug fit. Stretch labels, made from flexible materials, expand to fit containers, ensuring high visibility. ROSO labels, or roll-on-shrink-on labels, are applied in a roll format and shrink tightly upon heating. RF sleeve labels utilize radio frequency technology for applications requiring enhanced security and tamper evidence. Other market segments may include decorative or functional labels tailored to specific product needs.</p></p>
