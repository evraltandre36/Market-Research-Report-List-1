<p><p>The fertilizer market is primarily categorized into three types: nitrogen, phosphate, and others. Nitrogen fertilizers enhance plant growth by providing essential nutrients, commonly found in urea and ammonium nitrate. Phosphate fertilizers improve root development and flowering, derived from rock phosphate or processed sources. The "others" category includes potassium, micronutrients, and organic fertilizers, which contribute to overall soil health and sustainability. Each type plays a vital role in agricultural productivity, addressing specific nutrient deficiencies in crops for optimal yield.</p></p>
