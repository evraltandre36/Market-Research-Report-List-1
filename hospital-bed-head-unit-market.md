<p><p>The Hospital Bed Head Unit market comprises two primary types: Horizontal and Vertical Bed Head Units. Horizontal Bed Head Units are designed to be installed along the length of the bed, providing easy access to medical equipment and utilities for patient care. In contrast, Vertical Bed Head Units are mounted on the wall at the head of the bed, maximizing floor space while offering organized storage for medical equipment. Both types enhance the efficiency and functionality of hospital wards, contributing to improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
