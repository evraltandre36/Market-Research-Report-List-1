<p><ul><li>Skin Test</li><li>Allergy Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Latex allergy market types primarily include skin tests and allergy tests. Skin tests, such as prick and intradermal tests, are used to identify immediate allergic reactions to latex by exposing the skin to latex allergens and observing reactions. Allergy tests encompass a broader range, including blood tests like specific IgE tests that measure the immune response to latex proteins. Both testing methods are essential for diagnosing latex allergies, guiding treatment, and informing individuals about avoiding latex exposure in various settings.</p></p>
