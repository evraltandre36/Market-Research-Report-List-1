<p><p>The cooling tanks market comprises three primary types: Horizontal Closed Tanks, which store and cool liquids under pressure while minimizing contamination; Silo or Vertical Closed Tanks, designed for efficient space utilization and bulk storage, maintaining temperature control; and Open Tanks, which allow for more accessible cooling of liquids but may be prone to evaporation and contamination.
