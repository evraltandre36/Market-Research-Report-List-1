<p><p>The cotton terry blanket market can be divided into two primary types: woven and knitted. Woven blankets are made by interlacing warp and weft threads, resulting in a densely structured fabric that offers durability and a smooth finish. In contrast, knitted blankets utilize interconnected loops of yarn, providing a more elastic and soft texture, enhancing comfort. Both types showcase the absorbent properties of cotton terry fabric, making them ideal for warmth and versatility in various settings, such as homes and spas.</p></p>
