<p><p>The electronic prototyping market can be categorized into two main types: PCB prototyping and electronic product prototyping. PCB prototyping focuses on creating printed circuit boards to test designs and functionalities before mass production, facilitating rapid iterations. Electronic product prototyping encompasses the development of entire electronic devices, integrating hardware and software components to validate concepts, user experiences, and market viability. Both types support innovation and reduce time-to-market, enabling engineers and designers to refine and finalize their products efficiently.</p></p>
