<p><p>The bronze check valves market includes various connection types, primarily solder end connections and threaded end connections. Solder end connections involve joining the valve to piping systems using solder, providing a strong, leak-proof seal ideal for high-pressure applications. Threaded end connections, on the other hand, rely on threaded fittings to secure the valve, allowing for easier installation and maintenance. Both types cater to different installation needs and application environments, ensuring reliable flow control in various industries.</p></p>
