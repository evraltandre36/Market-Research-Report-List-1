<p><strong>The Raw Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxygen Process</li><li>Electric Process</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The raw steel market is primarily categorized into three types based on production methods: the Oxygen Process, the Electric Process, and Others. The Oxygen Process, also known as the basic oxygen steelmaking, utilizes molten iron and oxygen to remove impurities, making it efficient for large-scale production. The Electric Process, or electric arc furnace method, uses electricity to melt scrap steel, offering flexibility and lower emissions. The Others category includes alternative production methods such as direct reduced iron (DRI) and traditional blast furnaces, catering to diverse steel-making needs.</p></p>
