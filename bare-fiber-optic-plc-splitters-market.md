<p><p>Bare fiber optic PLC splitters are essential components in optical networks, allowing the distribution of signals from a single fiber to multiple outputs. The 1xN configuration features one input and multiple outputs, facilitating signal branching in various applications, such as FTTH networks. In contrast, the 2xN configuration provides two inputs that can be split among the same number of outputs, enhancing redundancy and reliability in network designs. Both types are widely used in telecommunications to optimize signal distribution and management.</p></p>
