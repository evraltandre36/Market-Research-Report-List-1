<p><p>The electromagnetic crane market comprises three main types: bridge, portal, and folding arm cranes. </p><p>Bridge cranes use a horizontal beam spanning between two supports, efficient for large areas. Portal cranes have a similar structure but are mounted on legs for ground-level operations, ideal for outdoor environments. Folding arm cranes feature articulated arms that can extend and retract, providing flexibility and access to tighter spaces. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, enhancing material handling capabilities in various applications.</p></p>
