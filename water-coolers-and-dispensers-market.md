<p><strong>The Water Coolers and Dispensers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bottled Water Cooler (BWC)</li><li>Point-of-Use (POU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water coolers and dispensers market is primarily divided into two types: Bottled Water Coolers (BWC) and Point-of-Use (POU) systems. BWC units utilize large bottles of water, which are replaced periodically, making them convenient for locations without direct water access. In contrast, POU systems are plumbed directly into the water supply, providing a continuous source of filtered water, reducing the need for bottle replacements. Both options cater to varying consumer preferences and environmental considerations.</p></p>
