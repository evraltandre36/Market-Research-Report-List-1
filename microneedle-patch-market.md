<p><strong>The Microneedle Patch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dissolving Type</li><li>Insoluble Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microneedle patch market is categorized into two main types: dissolving and insoluble. Dissolving microneedles are made from biodegradable materials that dissolve upon contact with the skin, releasing therapeutic agents for effective delivery. In contrast, insoluble microneedles are designed to deliver drugs through the skin while remaining intact, often requiring removal post-application. Both types cater to diverse medical applications, including vaccinations and pain management, leveraging their minimally invasive nature for enhanced patient compliance and improved therapeutic outcomes.</p></p>
