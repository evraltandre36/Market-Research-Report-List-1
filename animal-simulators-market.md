<p><ul><li>Whole Body</li><li>Part Body</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Animal simulators in the market are categorized into two primary types: whole body and part body simulators. Whole body simulators replicate an entire animal, providing comprehensive training for veterinary practices, handling, and surgical procedures. In contrast, part body simulators focus on specific anatomical sections, allowing for targeted learning and practice on particular skills, such as surgical techniques or physical examinations. Both types serve distinct educational purposes, enhancing the skill set of veterinarians and animal care professionals.</p></p>
