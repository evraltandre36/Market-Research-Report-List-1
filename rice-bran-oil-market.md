<p><strong>The Rice Bran Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extraction</li><li>Squeezing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rice Bran Oil market consists of two primary extraction types: solvent extraction and mechanical squeezing. Solvent extraction involves using chemicals to dissolve the oil from rice bran, offering higher yield and efficiency. In contrast, mechanical squeezing, or cold pressing, applies physical pressure to extract oil, retaining more nutrients and flavors. While solvent extraction dominates commercially due to its cost-effectiveness, mechanical squeezing appeals to health-conscious consumers seeking natural, minimally processed oils, thus influencing market dynamics and consumer preferences.</p></p>
