<p><ul><li>FPD X-Ray Inspection System</li><li>Film X-Ray Inspection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable X-Ray Inspection System market is primarily divided into two types: FPD (Flat Panel Detector) X-Ray Inspection Systems and Film X-Ray Inspection Systems. FPD systems utilize digital detector technology, offering high-resolution images and faster processing times, which facilitates real-time data analysis and reduces the need for developing films. In contrast, Film X-Ray Inspection Systems use traditional film-based methods, delivering a tangible output but requiring more time for image capture and development. Each system serves unique applications across various industries, including medical and industrial sectors.</p></p>
