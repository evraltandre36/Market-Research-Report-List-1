<p><p>The Analog RF Phase Shifter market consists of three main types: 180-degree phase shifters, 360-degree phase shifters, and those exceeding 360 degrees. </p><p>180-degree phase shifters allow for inversion of the signal, providing essential functionality in communication systems. 360-degree phase shifters enable full rotation of the signal phase, useful in diverse applications like beamforming. Phase shifters exceeding 360 degrees are often employed in advanced systems requiring multiple phase adjustments, enhancing flexibility in signal processing and transmission efficiency.</p></p>
