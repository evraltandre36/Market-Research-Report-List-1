<p><p>The industrial power tools market features various battery types, including lithium-ion, Ni-Cad, and NiMH batteries. Lithium-ion batteries are favored for their high energy density, lightweight design, and fast charging capabilities, making them ideal for demanding applications. Ni-Cad batteries offer reliability and robustness, although they have a lower energy capacity and can suffer from memory effect. NiMH batteries provide a balance between performance and environmental impact, boasting better capacity than Ni-Cad but less than lithium-ion. Each battery type caters to specific industrial needs and preferences.</p></p>
